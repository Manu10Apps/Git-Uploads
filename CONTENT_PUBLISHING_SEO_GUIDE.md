# Content Publishing SEO Checklist

**For Editorial Team & Content Creators**  
Updated: April 13, 2026

---

## ✅ PRE-PUBLISH CHECKLIST

### Article Basics

- [ ] **Title**: 50-60 characters, includes primary keyword, compelling
  - ✅ Example: `Rwanda Government Announces New Economic Policy - What This Means`
  - ❌ Avoid: `News`, `Important Announcement`, vague titles

- [ ] **Slug**: Lowercase, hyphenated, 3-5 words, includes keyword
  - ✅ Example: `/article/rwanda-economic-policy-2026`
  - ❌ Avoid: `/article/article-1234`, `/article/xyz-news`

- [ ] **Excerpt/Description**: 150-160 characters, natural language, keyword inclusion
  - ✅ Example: "Rwanda's finance ministry unveils comprehensive economic policy focusing on digital transformation and job creation for 2026-2030."
  - ❌ Avoid: "Read about the latest news from Rwanda"
  - ✏️ Tool: Use character counter to verify length

### Category & Metadata

- [ ] **Category Selected**: Must be from predefined list
  - Politics, Business, Technology, Investigations, Breaking, etc.
- [ ] **Author Name**: Full name, consistent across articles
  - Enables author authority for rankings

- [ ] **Featured Image**: 1200x630px (optimal OG size)
  - ✅ Do: Use high-quality, descriptive images
  - ❌ Don't: Use generic stock photos
  - 💡 Tip: Image alt text should be descriptive

### Content Structure

- [ ] **Headline (H1)**: Should match or be very similar to page title
  - Only ONE H1 per article
  - ✅ Include primary keyword naturally

- [ ] **Subheadings (H2/H3)**: Use hierarchy, include secondary keywords
  - ✅ Example structure:
    - H1: Main headline
    - H2: Key section
    - H3: Subsection
    - H2: Another key section

- [ ] **Introductory Paragraph**: 100-150 words, answers Who/What/When/Where/Why
  - Should include primary keyword naturally in first 100 words

- [ ] **Content Length**: 800-2000+ words for news articles
  - ✅ Longer-form content ranks better for competitive terms
  - ❌ Thin content (under 300 words) rarely ranks well

- [ ] **Word Breaks**: Use short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
  - Improves readability (UX = SEO ranking factor)
  - Makes content mobile-friendly

### Content Quality

- [ ] **Keywords Naturally Integrated**: 1-2 instances of primary keyword
  - ✅ Do: Primary keyword in title, intro, subheading, conclusion
  - ❌ Don't: Keyword stuffing (looks spammy)
  - The keyword should appear 1-2 times naturally, not forced

- [ ] **Fact Checked**: Verify all claims, statistics, quotes
  - Improves credibility (E-E-A-T: Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)

- [ ] **Up-to-Date**: Use current data, cite recent sources
  - News value is temporal—freshness matters

- [ ] **Unique Perspective**: Original analysis, not just regurgitation
  - Differentiate from competitors

### Internal Linking

- [ ] **Related Articles Linked**: 2-4 internal links with descriptive anchor text
  - ✅ Example: "Read our investigation on Rwanda's education reform"
  - ❌ Avoid: "Click here", "Read more"
  - Place naturally within content, not as footer

- [ ] **Linked to Category Page**: One link to main category page
  - Example: "More politics news from Rwanda"

### Publishing & Distribution

- [ ] **Publication Time**: Set appropriate timezone (Rwanda/Kigali timezone)
  - Impact on freshness signals

- [ ] **Share Text Ready**: Prepare 2-3 version of social share text
  - Different angles for different audiences
  - Include relevant hashtags

- [ ] **Meta Tags**: SEO Title and Description entered (if available)
  - Should be different from H1 in some cases for A/B testing

## 📋 KEYWORD SELECTION GUIDE

### Primary Keywords (Target 1 per article)

**What**: Main search query you want to rank for  
**How to Find**:

1. Use Google Search Console (show queries you rank for)
2. Look at related searches on Google
3. Use tools like Ubersuggest, SEMrush free tier
4. Think about what readers would search

**Examples for East Africa:**

- `Rwanda latest news`
- `Kenya inflation 2026`
- `Tanzania business news this week`
- `East Africa political news`

### Secondary Keywords (Include naturally if relevant)

- Related variations
- Long-tail versions (more specific)
- Synonyms

## 🔍 SEO BEST PRACTICES BY SECTION

### Breaking News Articles

- ✅ **Include publication time** (Google favors recent)
- ✅ **Update article** as story develops (adds freshness signal)
- ✅ **Link to related ongoing stories**
- ✅ **Use current, active language** ("is happening" not "happened")

### Investigations

- ✅ **Comprehensive, detailed content** (2000+ words)
- ✅ **Cite sources clearly** (builds authority)
- ✅ **Include relevant documents/evidence** if shareable
- ✅ **Link to background articles**
- 💡 Investigations are high-value content—promote heavily

### Analysis/Opinion

- ✅ **Clearly label as analysis/opinion**
- ✅ **Include author bio and credentials**
- ✅ **Back up claims with evidence**
- ✅ **Link to relevant facts/data**

## 🌍 MULTI-LANGUAGE OPTIMIZATION

### When Publishing in Multiple Languages:

- [ ] **Each version is unique, not just translated**
  - ✅ Do: Adapt examples, context for each audience
  - ❌ Don't: Machine translate

- [ ] **Language tag is correct**
  - Kinyarwanda (rw/ky)
  - English (en)
  - Swahili (sw)

- [ ] **Hreflang links included**
  - System handles automatically (verify in source)

- [ ] **URL structure includes language**
  - /en/article/slug or /sw/article/slug

## 📊 POST-PUBLISH MONITORING

### First 24 Hours

- [ ] Check Google Search Console for errors
- [ ] Verify all links work (test internal links, external links)
- [ ] Confirm article appears in sitemap
- [ ] Monitor social shares
- [ ] Check Analytics for initial traffic

### First Week

- [ ] Monitor SERPs for keyword position (use GSC)
- [ ] Check CTR—if low, optimize title/description
- [ ] Monitor engagement (time on page, bounce rate)
- [ ] Consider internal link additions if not ranking

### First Month

- [ ] Article should appear in Google index
- [ ] Driving some organic search traffic
- [ ] May be ranking #10-50+ for main keyword (early days)

### Ongoing

- [ ] Update article if new relevant info emerges
- [ ] Add "Updated: [date]" if revised
- [ ] Monitor for questions in comments—consider updating content
- [ ] Track month-over-month performance

## ⚠️ COMMON SEO MISTAKES TO AVOID

| Mistake                   | Impact                 | Fix                                       |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Thin content (<300 words) | Won't rank             | Expand to 800+ words                      |
| Keyword stuffing          | Penalized              | Use keyword 1-2x naturally                |
| No internal links         | Lower SEO value        | Add 2-4 relevant internal links           |
| Poor meta description     | Low CTR from SERP      | Write compelling 150-160 char description |
| No category specified     | Poor organization      | Select relevant category                  |
| Duplicate of other site   | Penalized              | Ensure originality or use canonical       |
| All caps in title         | Poor CTR               | Use normal capitalization                 |
| Future date published     | Freshness signal lost  | Use current date for timely content       |
| Broken links              | Poor UX, lost SEO      | Test all links before publishing          |
| No image                  | Less appealing in SERP | Add 1200x630px image                      |

## 🚀 ADVANCED: FEATURED SNIPPET OPTIMIZATION

**Want to appear in Position 0 (Featured Snippet)?**

### Criteria for Different Types:

**Definition/Paragraph Snippets:**

- Clear opening sentence (50-60 words) that answers the question
- Include keyword in the definition
- ✅ Example: "Amakuru Agezweho means 'Breaking News' in Kinyarwanda. This refers to news that is recent, important, and still developing. Breaking news is given priority coverage because of its significance to the public."

**List Snippets:**

- Numbered or bulleted lists (5-10 items)
- Should answer "How to...", "What are...", "Best ways to..."
- ✅ Example: "Ways to verify news credibility: 1. Check the source... 2. Look for..."

**Table Snippets:**

- Compare two or more items
- Clear column and row headers
- ✅ Example: Comparison table of inflation rates across East African countries
